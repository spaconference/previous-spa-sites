Conference Fees

This series of conferences has always represented extremely good value for money and this is especially true this year with our change of venue.  For the first time this year, the event is NOT residential and so participants will need to arrange their own accommodation or travel to the conference each day.  

Without a doubt the best value can be gained from the conference by attending all four days.  However the change to a nonresidential format makes it possible for us to offer registration for just one or two days of the conference. The Sunday sessions are free to anyone attending any part of the conference

The conference fee gives entrance to the full programme of conference sessions, lunch and evening diversions on the day booked. 

Prices for the SPA2009 conference start from just £190 + VAT.

United Kingdom Visa Requirements

Due to the limited number of places available on this event, where a delegate would need a UK Visa to enter the UK, the booking will only be accepted upon receipt of proof that the delegate is already in possession of a valid UK Visa. We reserve the right to verify any delegates immigration status prior to accepting a booking.

No letters of invitation or similar will be issued to any delegate. The organisers will not under any circumstances act as a sponsor to any individual for the purposes of obtaining a UK Visa. Information on UK Visa can be found at this UK Government website
