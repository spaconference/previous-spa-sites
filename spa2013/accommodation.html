Accommodation Options

As SPA2013 is NOT a residential conference, participants will need to either make a hotel booking or travel to the event each day. While we are not able to make any specific hotel recommendations participants may wish to note that our evening activities are taking place at The Strand Palace Hotel (http://www.strandpalacehotel.co.uk).  The hotel is located directly opposite the rear exit of BCS London.

Alternative hotels in the area are:

Imperial Hotels

The Imperial, President and Bedford hotels (see www.imperialhotels.co.uk) are reasonable compared to the Strand Palace and only 5 minutes away - visiting BCS staff often stay at the President (£77) which is both cheap and clean.

Travel Lodge, Covent Garden
 Budget hotel chain in walking distance of BCS London.  Hotel has two sites one at North End of Drury Lane and one across road on High Holburn. More info at http://www.travelodge.co.uk/search_and_book/hotel_overview.php?hotel_id=318.

Waldorf Hilton
http://www.hilton.co.uk/waldorf.

Saco Covent Garden, Serviced Apartments
Serviced apartments near venue, book via http://www.bookapartmentsinlondon.co.uk.
