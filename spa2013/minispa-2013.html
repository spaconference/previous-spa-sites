About the SPA Conference

The SPA Conference is a different kind of conference. Our attendees keep coming back year after year because SPA is a conference that focuses on interactivity and discussion, a place for people in the software industry to gather to reflect, exchange ideas and learn. SPA is a conference where you meet brilliant people and come away buzzing with new ideas.
