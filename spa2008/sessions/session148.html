SPA Conference session: Real Options in a Nutshell

One-line description:Real Options will change your decision making process for ever.
 
Session format: Tutorial (75 mins) [read about the different session types]
 
Abstract:This session introduces the subject of Real Options and how they can be applied to virtually any decision that needs to be made.

This session is about changing peoples mindset and behavior. Started out as a quest to price real options, we found a rational decision making process based on maths and logic rather than emotion, feelings and intuition.

Leaving this session people discover options where they have not seen them before. Decision making after this session will never be the same again.


 
Audience background:All can attend. Anyone who makes decisions. Previous audiences include Managers, Project Managers, Developers, Artists, Nurses, Business Consultants and Management Consultants
 
Benefits of participating:Leaving this session people discover options where they have not seen them before. Decision making after this session will never be the same again.
 
Materials provided:Slides and material needed for the game we run at the start of the session.
 
Process:Both presenters are experienced in organizing interactive presentations and discussions. Although we say 'Presentation' and we use some slides as placeholders, the way we work is highly interactive and includes games, presentation, discussion , case studies
 
Detailed timetable:00:00 Session start
00:00-00:05 Introduction & Game instructions
00:05-00:15 Game
00:15-01:15 Presentation / Discussion / Case Studies
01:15 Session end

We have no detailed planning in this session, because we depend on the audience engaging us and as a result we don't determine the timetable upfront.
 
Outputs:None, other than a continuing discussion on decision making and risk management.
 
History:This presentation has been delivered over ten times to audiences varying from a few colleagues at work to 40 attendees at XPDay06. The presentation has been adapted as further insight has been acquired into the material.
