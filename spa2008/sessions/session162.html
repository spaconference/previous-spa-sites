SPA Conference session: Developing for Social Networking Sites

One-line description:Understanding Facebook etc from a developers point of view
 
Session format: Case study (75 mins) [read about the different session types]
 
Abstract:With many of the Social Networking sites (Facebook, MySpace, Bebo, Plaxo) opening up their platforms to third party developers, this session focuses on the hows and whys of developing for these sites.

Using examples from a real-world user-funded project for Facebook this session presents an overview to developers of:

a) Why and how these sites are opening up their platforms
b) What sort of applications can be developed and the kind of code needed to do so
c) The presenters view of where all this might be leading
 
Audience background:The session is focused on developers not familiar with this sort of technology but might also be of interest to those with a less "hands-on" role who are trying to get a handle on what social network sites might mean for their business/organisation. A basic understanding of web technology (i.e. you know what a HTTP GET or POST is) is required and it will make a lot more sense if you have used at least one social network site such as Facebook, LinkedIn, Bebo or Myspace. The ability to at least read PHP code and make some sense of it would also help you get the most out of the session
 
Benefits of participating:* To get a "jump start" in being able to develop for Facebook and similar sites
* To form an opinion on where social networking site might be taking software development
* To be better equiped to judge if this is a technology that impacts on your organisation/business
 
Materials provided:* Slide copies
* Code samples
* URL reference lists
 
Process:Mostly the session will be a mixture of presentation and demonstration. However those participating in the session will be asked to reflect in a brief exercise how there organisation might approach Social Networking applications - as either an opportunity or a threat
 
Detailed timetable:00:00 - 00:10 Introduction including what we mean by Social Networking site
00:10 - 00:15 A brief history lesson
00:15 - 00:55 Case Study: Facebook "iSmile" project - presentations and demo to illustrate Facebook Architecture and APIs
00:55 - 01:05 Group exercise: What do social networking applications mean for me
01:05 - 01:10 Pointers to other things you might need to know
01:10 - 1:15 Where might all this be going
 
Outputs:Presenters will write up on a SPA wiki the outcomes of the discussions question.
 
History:This is a new session
