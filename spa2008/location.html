Location

SPA2008 will take place at the Robinson Centre, St.Neots, Bedfordshire, UK.

The conference session rooms and main accommodation are provided as part of the excellent facilities offered by the Robinson Centre. Meals and accommodation are of a high standard and are included in the price of the conference. Given the intensive and highly interactive nature of the conference, we are able to offer a fantastic rate that includes accommodation and all meals, allowing you to stay onsite for the whole of the conference. Book early to avoid missing out on the onsite accommodation.
