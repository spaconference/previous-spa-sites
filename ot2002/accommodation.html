Accommodation at OT2002
Bed, breakfast, and dinner are provided at Christ Church college, where a variety of informal working sessions, gatherings, and diversions occur in the evening.

Accommodation is included in the conference fee, and is available from Sunday night until Wednesday. An extra night may be booked if required.

Enquiries may be made to the Conference Office: +44 207 344 5732
