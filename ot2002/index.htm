 The OT conference is the liveliest exchange of the latest ideas and practical skills in software architecture, design and development.

...where the experts learn

'The OT conference is where experts gather to learn' says John Nolan, this year's conference chair. 'It is set up specifically to circulate know-how, but without the sales bias you get at trade shows, and without the theoretical flavour of some conferences. Most sessions are interactive — we believe you learn best by doing.'

OT has an interactive style that clearly distinguishes it from marketing-led, vendor-driven conferences. Working in a lively and stimulating environment, you'll learn new techniques and share your experience with other developers.
