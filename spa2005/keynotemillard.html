Keynote speech

Digital Tears: Machines with Emotion

Nicola Millard (BT)

As our relationships with technology grow ever deeper, will there come a time where machines can start to sympathise with you? The talk will look at the role of emotion in human communication and decision making and how (and if) emotional machines can be designed to enhance the ways in which we use technology both for leisure and in the workplace.
