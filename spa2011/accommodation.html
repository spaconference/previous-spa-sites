Accommodation Options

As SPA2011 is NOT a residential conference, participants will need to either make a hotel booking or travel to the event each day. While we are not able to make any specific hotel recommendations participants may wish to note that our evening activities are taking place at The Strand Palace Hotel (http://www.strandpalacehotel.co.uk).  The hotel is located directly opposite the rear exit of BCS London.

Hotels in the area are:

Strand Palace Hotel
SPA2011 activities take place in this hotel.

Club Singles @ £110+VAT
Club Doubles @ £135+VAT

Imperial Hotels

The Imperial, President and Bedford hotels (see www.imperialhotels.co.uk) are reasonable compared to the Strand Palace and only 5 minutes away - visiting BCS staff often stay at the President (£77) which is both cheap and clean.
