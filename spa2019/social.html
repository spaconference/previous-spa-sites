Evening diversions

Dinner and the evening activities will take place at the conference venue from 18:00 onwards on Monday 24th and Tuesday 25th June.

Monday evening diversions

Beer Tasting and talk from James Atherton from The Beerblefish Brewing Company.

Tuesday evening diversions

"The Unbelievable Knuth". A panel show based on "The Unbelievable Truth" radio show, in which each panelist prepares a short talk on a subject of their choice. The talk should be entirely untruthful, except for 5 statements of truth. The panelist delivers the talk while the other panelists interrupt if they think they have spotted a truth. If they are correct they win a point, otherwise they loose a point, and any undetected truths score for the presenter.

If you think you can do better than our esteemed panel let us know - we are always up for another person on the panel
