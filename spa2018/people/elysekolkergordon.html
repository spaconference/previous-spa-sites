Elyse Kolker Gordon runs the growth engineering team at Strava. Previously, she was director of web engineering at Vevo, where she regularly solved challenges with isomorphic apps. She is also the author of Isomorphic Web Applications: https://www.manning.com/books/isomorphic-web-applications. Outside of work, she loves learning new things, traveling and music (including playing the drums).
