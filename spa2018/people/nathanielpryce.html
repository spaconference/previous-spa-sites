Nat Pryce

Technemetis Ltd.

Nat Pryce is an independent consultant with expertise in software design, development process & practices who works mostly in finance and telecoms.

He is co-author, with Steve Freeman, of the book ''Growing Object-Oriented Software, Guided by Tests'' and has developed various libraries and tools for test-driven development, including jMock, Hamcrest, WindowLicker and Team Piazza.

He was a co-organiser of the first XP Day conference and joint programme chair of XP Day 2004 and 2005. He has presented at OT and SPA several times.
