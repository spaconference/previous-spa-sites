Tamara Lopez

The Open University

Tamara Lopez is a Research Associate in the School of Computing and Communications at The Open University. She conducts empirical research using qualitative methods to examine software development practice. Her most recent publication is "An Investigation of Security Conversations in Stack Overflow" given at the SEAD workshop held as part of the International Conference of Software Engineering in May, 2018.
