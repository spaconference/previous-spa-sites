SPA Conference session: Thinking Tools for Software Developers

One-line description:Learn how to combine Mind Maps. Thinking Hats and Action Shoes
 
Session format: Tutorial (150 mins) [read about the different session types]
 
Abstract:As software developers, we spend much of our time planning, learning, communicating and reflecting.

This session will look at three tools that can help us do these more effectively. We will cover the theory and practice of Tony Buzan's Mind Mapping techniquque. We will aso explore how Mind Mapping can be enhanced by combining it with other Thinking Tools. These include Edward de Bono's 6 Thinking Hats and 6 Action shoes.

You will learn how and when to Mind Map, and practise ways of making your maps easier to create and easier to understand. You will also explore Thinking Process Models and find out how to apply what you have learned to the software development process.
 
Audience background:Whther you are an interested novice or an experienced Mind Mapper, you should find this session valuable and fun!
 
Benefits of participating:- learn the key elements of Mind Mapping
- identify new applications of the technique
- use colour conventions to enhance the value of your maps
- apply appropriate thinking processes to problem solving, information capture and decision making
 
Materials provided:Reference materials
Pens and paper
 
Process:Presentations and Exercises, individually and in small groups
