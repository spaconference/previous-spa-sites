SPA Conference session: Architecting Large-Scale Systems

One-line description:Examing the architectural challenges in creating large-scale software systems
 
Session format: Tutorial (75 mins) [read about the different session types]
 
Abstract:Many initiatives, such as systems engineering, enterprise
architecture, strategic reuse programmes and Service-Oriented Architecture
(SOA) require us to move beyond a "single system" mentality. In this
session, Peter will examine some of the architectural challenges in
working in such environments and explore some of the techniques that have
been successfully applied to address these challenges. Peter will focus on
the role of the architect in working in such environments, but will also
touch on other disciplines, such as project management
