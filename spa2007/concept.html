The conference for participants

SPA2006 brings together software development practitioners to exchange and develop the latest ideas and skills in software architecture, design and development.

Mar 16-19, 2007, Bedfordshire, England

Explore a broad range of subjects from leading-edge technology, through pioneering software development and deployment practices, to innovative techniques for managing software projects and the people that make up the project team.

Come learn from industry experts and from each other, hear about real world case studies, and delve into hands-on code examples. This is a great opportunity to find new inspiration for your work.

Take part in four action-packed days. SPA2006 is for people that want to share experiences, work together to address hot topics, and ask tough questions. Most sessions stress participation and learning - you don't need to know the answers to join in, you just need enthusiasm. Whether you're interested in the latest technology, pioneering development practices or innovative project management techniques SPA2006 will have something valuable to offer you.

SPA2006 is the annual conference of the BCS Software Practice Advancement Specialist Group, and is one of the UK's foremost software events. SPA2006 continues the 13-year tradition of the world-famous SPA conferences and their direct predecessors, the OT series.
