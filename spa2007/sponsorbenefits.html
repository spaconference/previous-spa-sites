Sponsorship opportunities

Leading IT organisations and companies with forward-thinking IT departments can be high-profile participants at this exciting event by becoming conference sponsors.

SPA is the foremost conference on contemporary software practice.Now in its 15th year, this event attracts many participants who are experts in their fields, and who often play a leading role in product and service recommendation and acquisition: our audience comprises both technical software specialists and IT management.

In addition, the conference attracts prominent industry figures: In recent years those participating have included Norman Kerth, Dave Thomas, Ivar Jacobson, Martin Fowler, Joshua Bloch and Alistair Cockburn. This conference is firmly established as the event at which new ideas and fresh thinking on software practice emerge.

SPA2007 will explore a broad range of subjects from leading-edge technology, through pioneering software development and deployment practices, to innovative techniques for managing projects and the people that make up the project team.

Becoming a sponsor of SPA2007 provides the highest possible profile at the event.

Sponsorship benefits include:

  • Prominent billing as a sponsor in all promotional activities and materials.
  • Billing in Press Releases.
  • Distribution of brochures and personalised invitations on your behalf to your clients and prospective customers.
  • Prominent display stand and demonstration area at the conference
  • Speaking slot in the conference programme.
  • Four conference places with full accommodation.
  • Your logo on items given to all conference participants.
  • Your marketing and other promotional materials inserted into participant packs
  • Delegate list with contact details
