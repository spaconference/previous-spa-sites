Conference Fees

This series of conferences has always represented extremely good value for money.

The conference fee gives entrance to the full programme of conference sessions and evening diversions from Sunday to Wednesday (inclusive). The conference is residential and therefore all accommodation/meals are included in the conference fees.

Prices including accommodation for the SPA2007 conference start from just £1150 + VAT.

Rates for SPA2007 Rate
SPA SG or BCS Members £1150
Non-Member £1200

Discounts available for group bookings! Call 0870 760 6863 for details.
All fees are subject to VAT at 17.5%.
Accommodation and meals are included in the conference fees. Non-residential rates are not available.

Accommodation and meals

Accommodation usually consists of three nights' en-suite accommodation at Homerton College with all meals provided. Delegates traveling long distances have the option of booking a further nights accommodation on Saturday or Wednesday. (It is also possible to book for 3 nights now and add a further nights stay once travel arrangements have been made).

Additional Accommodation options for SPA2007 Rate
Additional night Bed & Breakfast (Saturday or Wednesday) £58
All rates are subject to VAT at 17.5%

Read the terms and conditions of bookings (including an explanation of why we are not able to offer non-residential prices).


United Kingdom Visa Requirements

Participants who need to obtain a UK Visa to allow them to attend the conference should be aware that following advice from the UK Home Office the organisers are not able to issue formal letters of invitation or similar until all monies for conference fees have been received by bank transfer.
