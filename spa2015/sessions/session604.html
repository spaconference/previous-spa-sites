SPA Conference session: User Story "Call My Bluff"

One-line description:Can you guess which of the explanations of a user story is the "real" one?
 
Session format: Diversion (or Workshop) [read about the different session types]
 
Abstract:User stories are almost ubiquitous, but unfortunately their quality is not guaranteed. In this session our 'celebrity' panel will each provide a humorous explanation of a user story (harvested from the real world). Your job is to decide which of the explanations is correct.

This won't be exactly like the original TV game, because the purpose is to demonstrate that you are more likely to really understand a user story if you question it. And finally to demonstrate that detailed, concrete questions (using examples) are more likely to help you understand than imprecise, abstract questions.

 
Audience background:Any one that has worked with user stories (or requirements)
 
Benefits of participating:Fun
Practical demonstration of the power of enquiry
 
Materials provided:User stories
Panelists
 
Process:Panel show with host. Audience in theatre or cabaret layout
 
Detailed timetable:00:00 - 00:05 Intro
00:05 - 00:10 Panellists 'explain' the user story and audience votes on correct answer
00:10 - 00:20 Audience are allowed to ask general, abstract questions of panel. Vote again.
00:20 - 00:30 Audience are allowed to ask specific, concrete questions of panel. Vote again.
00:30 - 00:35 Correct answer revealed. Examination of how voting changes correlated with correctness
00:35 - 01:05 Repeat
01:05 - 01:15 Wrap up
 
Outputs:None
 
History:It ran on telly for 30 years.
