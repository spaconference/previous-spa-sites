SPA Bloggers

SPA prides itself on the being the most interactive of conferences: attendees are encouraged to share experiences and work together to improve their knowledge. Many of the conference's alumni carry on this interaction throughout the year, in many forums, including the use of blogs.

Amongst those actively blogging about the SPA Conference and related issues are:
  • Willem van den Ende
  • Jason Gorman
  • Rachel Davies
  • James Roberston
  • Kevin Rutherford
  • Andy Pols
  • Pascal van Cauwenberghe
  • Marc Evers
  • Steve Freeman
  • David Draper
Reading these might give you a flavour of the kind of people you might expect to encounter at SPA2006.

Note to bloggers: if you're intending to attend SPA2006, and are blogging about the conference, and would like to appear on this list, please contact the organisers.
