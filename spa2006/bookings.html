Conference Fees

This series of conferences has always represented extremely good value for money.

The conference fee gives entrance to the full programme of conference sessions and evening diversions from Sunday to Wednesday (inclusive). The conference is residential and accommodation/meals will be booked and charged to you at the same time as the conference booking. Prices for accommodation/meals are shown separately at the bottom of this page.
