Location

SPA2010 will take place in the excellent facilities provided by The British Computer Society's London Office.

Located in the heart of London's West End, just a few moments walk from Covent Garden, the venue is easy to reach by public transport and is located near a wide range of hotels.

 

The full address of the venue is

BCS London Office - Southampton Street First Floor,
The Davidson Building,
5 Southampton Street
London
WC2E 7HA

Tel: 01793 417666 Fax: 01793 417669

Full joining instructions including maps and information on how best to travel to the conference are available here.

Once your are in Southampton Street, just look out for the very distinctive clock over the doorway to the The Davidson Building. 

When you have signed in with security, please head up to the first floor where the conference will take place.
