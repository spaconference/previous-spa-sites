Accommodation Options

As SPA2010 is NOT a residential conference, participants will need to either make a hotel booking or ravel to the event each day. While we are not able to make any specific hotel recommendations participants may wish to note that our evening activities are taking place at The Strand Palace Hotel (http://www.strandpalacehotel.co.uk).  The hotel is located directly opposite the rear exit of BCS London.  The Strand Palace is currently offering SPA attendees their their "best rates" for May (£110 single, £160 double, inc VAT and breakfast).
