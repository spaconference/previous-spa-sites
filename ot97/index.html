OT97: Web Posters


The OT conferences have always been enjoyed and acclaimed for their exciting, participative style. No snoring in front of droning lecturers here! Nearly every session involves real exchange of ideas, practical work, and discussion. And there are many small discussion groups --- "BOFs" --- that you organise, at the conference, on whatever topic you're most concerned with.


But this year, we're starting before we get there!


Hot topics --- the posters

Find out in advance what will be the big issues being debated at OT97. Scan the posters; join the debate --- and hit the ground running at the conference!

Your invitation to make a poster for OT97

The poster catalogue was primed by our scheduled session leaders. But everyone is invited to put one up. Outline your best success, your biggest headache, your newest technique, or favorite tool, or just a crazy idea. See what electronic reactions you get --- and continue the discussion at OT97!

See you at Oxford in April!
