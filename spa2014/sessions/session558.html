SPA Conference session: Bounce

One-line description:How to Harness Your Resilience in a Changing World
 
Session format: Workshop [read about the different session types]
 
Abstract:Are you feeling stressed? Do you feel uncertain about the future? Everyday we find ourselves facing different challenges, accomplishing various tasks and constantly adapting.

As mankind has evolved, we've become more conscious and informed of who we are and how our minds work. Resilience, previously considered a personality trait, is now a vital modern-life skill which can be developed to help us better deal with everyday challenges as well as great adversity.

Join us to better understand how resilient you are and figure out how to become more resilient to overcome your next big challenge.

OBJECTIVES
Understand what resilience is
Strengthen your resilience by becoming conscious of how resilient you are
Come up with ideas to become more resilient

 
Audience background:No prior knowledge required
Open-mindedness
A wiliness to reflect deeply and improve
A desire to be more adaptable and robust
 
Benefits of participating:An understanding of what resilience is
Increased self-awareness of your resilience
Ideas for empowering yourself and others
 
Materials provided:Presentation, stationery for exercises, sweets
 
Process:Combination of individual, small group and larger group exercises
