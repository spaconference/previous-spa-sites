SPA Conference session: Hands on Hybrid Applications with MongoDB and Hadoop

One-line description:Hands on Hybrid Applications with MongoDB and Hadoop
 
Session format: Tutorial (75 mins) [read about the different session types]
 
Abstract:Business moves fast, very fast. Its driven by making decision based on facts, but how do you deal with facts that are hours or days old? Periodic data warehouse loads and ETL process no longer scale to meet the increasing business needs in the web economy.

This session starts of by describing why we now have many different approaches to storing data and how to identify use cases for noSQL, RDBMS and warehousing. From this introduction we continue by hands on a use case and a live application that uses a hybrid of MongoDB and Hadoop. We then move on to labs sessions that look at the challenges of horizontal scalability and an technical deep dive into scaling an hybrid application.
 
Audience background:This talk is aimed at Enterprise or Data Architectures with basic experience with MongoDB and Hadoop
 
Benefits of participating:Gain hands on experience with hybrid applications with MongoDB and Hadoop
 
Materials provided:MongoDB
 
Process:This session has structured exercises that starts with a simple MongoDB installation and then builds to integrate Hadoop
