Participation fees

Fees are in UK pounds and include VAT at 17.5%.

Participation fees include all conference documentation, accommodation, breakfast, luncheon, dinner, and diversions. The reduced fees shown for Pre December 2000 apply only to bookings for which payment is received by the OT2001 office before 1 December 2000.
Payment Payment for registration is required in Sterling in advance of the Conference, and can be made by cheque or money order made payable to IEE and crossed or by Credit Card. Credit facilities will only be considered upon receipt of an official order.
Cancellations In the event of cancellation, and provided that written notice is received within seven days prior to the event, a refund of 75% of the registration fee can be made. If no notice of cancellation is received, no refund can be made. No refunds can be made in respect of the charges for accommodation and social events. Delegates will be liable for services booked but not paid for.
Accommodation Conference participants are encouraged to use the residential facilities offered, in order to enjoy informal meetings and discussions outside the organised conference sessions. The costs for the facilities are included in the participation fees given aboce. Full details of the facilities, which incllude accommodation in student quarters and meals, are send with confirmation of your booking.
