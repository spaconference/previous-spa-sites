Painting the Business Model Canvas

workshop, 150min

Abstract

From engineers to executives, successful businesses bring disparate disciplines together. How can people with such different skills and backgrounds communicate efficiently and effectively, and still focus on delighting their customers?

This session will make you question everything you thought you knew about the business you work in and give you skills to build a more realistic business model.
