Servant Leadership - moving the power from me to we

This session introduces the topic of Servant Leadership - a proven , practical and progressive approach to managing yourself and leading others

60 min interactive session - 5

Abstract

In this session Angie will bring alive the practical and proven principles of Servant Leadership . She will invite participants to interact , discuss their experience of leadership today while setting the scene for the opportunity ( and challenges ) of adopting different ways of working as a part of the journey to a true organisational agility. This session will help you understand what servant leadership is, why it's a great fit for many organisations and how you as an invidious like can adopt servant leadership practices .

Why not join this session and explore the way you can assess your own approach to and leadership mindset , this session is a perfect complement to increasing your technical skill set

Audience background

None , ideally would be in direct or indirect ( matrix ) leadership position

Benefits of participating

Participants receive a nifty self assessment to help audit their own leadership skills - materials that regardless of industry or tenure will help focus on development areas .
Other benefits include a ground level understanding of a leadership framework that's growing in popularity and presence.

Materials provided

8 page self assessment guide , looks blinking funky !
I will present using slides and material to facilitate an interactive session

Process

Leader delivery and groups of 10-15 . Ideal session size will not exceed 75 people

Detailed timetable

60 min session
10 min intro
30 mins group work
10 mins learning points
5 min introduction to self assessment materials and further reading.
5 min continuity ( start late etc )

Outputs

Web page ( in development ) plus 8 page self assessment as a hard copy.
Mail to participants with link to further material and reading and our fab servant leader intro films .
