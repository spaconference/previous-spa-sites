Sponsorship opportunities

SPA attracts many participants who are experts in their fields, and who often play a leading role in product and service recommendation and acquisition.

Becoming a sponsor of SPA2017 provides the highest possible profile at the event.

Sponsorship Benefits

General Sponsorship - £1250 + 20% VAT (Three Available)

Be a core supporter of the SPA conference and gain prominent placement of your organisation and message to conference attendees leading up to and throughout the conference.

  • Large logo on the conference website
  • Placement of a short promotional message on the SPA conference website
  • Thanks and acknowledgement on conference social media channels
  • Public thanks to the sponsor at conference plenary sessions
  • Opportunity to give a 10 minute "lightning talk" at a plenary session
  • Opportunity to display a banner or have a small stand in the shared area throughout the conference (Two Available)
  • Your marketing and other promotional materials inserted into participant packs
  • Delegate list with contact details

Dinner Package - £1000 + 20% VAT (Two Available)

Sponsor one of the conference dinners and gain focused attention from the conference's attendees in a friendly an relaxed environment, as they are gathered for a sociable evening meal.

  • Public thanks to the sponsor at conference plenary sessions
  • Opportunity to run a "brown bag" session for the conference attendees directly before or after dinner
  • Opportunity to display sponsor banners during the dinner event
  • Logo on the conference website.

Lunch Package - £750 + 20% VAT (Three Available)

Sponsor one of the conference lunches and gain attention from attendees as they are gathered together for one of SPA's buffet lunches, where attendees mingle and share their experiences of the conference that morning.

  • Public thanks to the sponsor at conference plenary sessions
  • Opportunity to run a "brown bag" session for the conference attendees during the lunch break
  • Opportunity to display sponsor banners during the lunch break
  • Logo on the conference website

Contact us to discuss how sponsoring SPA2017 can benefit your organisation.
