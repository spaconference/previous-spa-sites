OT2003 Sponsorship Package

OT is the foremost conference on contemporary software practice. Now in its 11th year, this event regularly attracts between 150 and 250 participants, the vast majority of whom play a leading role in product recommendation and acquisition: our audience comprises both technical software specialists and IT management.

In addition, the conference attracts prominent industry figures: for example, over the past three years, participants included Bruce Anderson, Kent Beck, Dick Gabriel, Frank Buschmann, James Coplien, John Daniels, Ian Graham, and Steve Vinoski. This conference is firmly established as the event at which new ideas and fresh thinking on software practice emerge.

OT2003 will explore a broad range of subjects from leading-edge technology, through pioneering software development and deployment practices, to innovative techniques for managing projects and the people that make up the project team.

Becoming a sponsor of OT2003 provides the highest possible profile at the event.

Previous sponsors of this event include Rational, Bauer, Lucent, Hitachi, IBM, Iona Technologies, Kennedy Carter, KPMG, Object Designers, Objective Computer Systems, Project Technology International, QA Training, The Object People, Versant, Valtech, TogetherSoft.
