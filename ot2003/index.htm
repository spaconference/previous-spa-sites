 The OT conference is the liveliest exchange of the latest ideas and practical skills in software architecture, design and development.

...where the experts learn

The OT conference is where experts gather to learn about the latest methods and techniques in software development. We circulate know-how, but without the sales bias you get at trade shows, and without the theoretical flavour of some conferences. Most sessions are interactive — we believe you learn best by doing.

Above all, OT regulars find software development fun and productive — they get the job done and enjoy doing it.

Take a look at this year's programme to see how you can improve your skills and your team's productivity.
