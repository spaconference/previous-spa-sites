Accommodation at OT2003
OT2003 will be held at Robinson College Executive Centre near St.Neot's, Cambridgeshire, UK. It is about 50 minutes by rail from London, and about 1 hour by car from Stansted airport. Directions.

Bed, breakfast, and dinner are provided at the venue and included in the price of the conference. Accommodation is available from dinner on Sunday night until lunch on Wednesday. An extra night may be booked if required.

Enquiries may be made to the Conference Office: +44 207 344 5732
