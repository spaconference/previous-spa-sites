Hold a BoF Session at OT2003

The formal conference programme is now under construction, so more proposals won't be invited until next year.

But you can still hold an informal "Birds of a Feather" session at the conference.

BoF sessions are an essential part of what gives this working, interactive conference its unique character. They are organised at the conference itself. You don't have to be an expert --- you just need a topic you want to discuss. Many of the most memorable sessions, and some of the most effective at forging lasting working relationships, have been BoFs held in peripheral meeting rooms --- or, of course, the bar.

Dates

  • 1 December 2002 -- formal programme finalised
  • 3 January 2003 -- discounted conference booking closes
  • 20 January 2003 -- programme session leaders' materials for conference documentation sent to sessions@ot2003.org
  • 30 March 2003 --- Working Groups
  • 31 March -- 2 April 2003 --- OT2003 --- including several slots for BoF sessions
