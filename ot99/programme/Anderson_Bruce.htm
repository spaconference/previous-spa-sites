Architecture as Process

Workshop      150 minutes

Bruce Anderson and Martin Cooke

 
  • To give people an expanded awareness and wider concept of software architecture
  • for people who have already built a system or two and in any number
  • structured into: solve a problem in small groups; get new information/ideas; re-solve the problem; what I learned plus feedback with handouts for problem (in conference binder) and new information (handed out at session)
  • run as a combination of small-group and plenary working
  • producing a "what we learned" poster for the conference and paragraph for the journal
  • led by Bruce Anderson (gent) and Martin Cooke (architect, abstractionist, meta-maker)
