Software is Software

Getting Beyond the Horseless Carriage

Wednesday 9:00am    Keynote

Kent Beck

Thinking about developing software has been dominated by paradigms borrowed from other disciplines. We now have enough experience developing software that we can begin to appreciate it for itself, as a unique human activity different than any other. In this talk I will step away from my usual brutally practical public persona and talk about the philosophy of software development.

The Presenter

Kent Beck hates writing these pseudo-third-person biographies. If he talks about his accomplishments, like the HotDraw and xUnit frameworks, the articles, the books (The Smalltalk Best Practice Patterns, Refactoring (with Martin Fowler), Extreme Programming: Embracing Change), and the cool projects, he feels like a chest-thumper. If he's too modest, however, you might not come to his talk, which would disappoint him.
