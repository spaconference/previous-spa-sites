Process patterns

Alan Cameron Wills

Trireme

Patterns can be used to describe the method of software development, as well as designs. They help to modularise and put into a taxonomy the variety of methodological ideas currently found in methods, texts, and papers.

What are the principal patterns one would expect to find in a language of process patterns? This working group will survey well-known development methods, and try to integrate them into a structure of patterns.

Key topics

Scope and content of process patterns

When and how to use process patterns

Decoupling aspects of the process

Combining process patterns

Tool support
